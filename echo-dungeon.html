ECHO DUNGEON

The dungeon is utterly dark. You see nothing — until you move. Every step sends out a wave of sound that briefly paints the walls, treasure, and the things hunting you. Between pulses there is only black. Navigate by sound and memory.

Find the glowing sigil, then reach the exit portal to descend deeper. Avoid the red echoes.
